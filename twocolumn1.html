About Me

Welcome to my website! I have very strong opinions about the future of legal services, and want to develop my ideas in public. Be prepared to read about subjects including, but not limited to: Science Fiction, The Sahel, Self-Help, Travel, and my quest for the best hand-pulled noodles in Boston.

Stay tuned for more updates! This project is a work in progress, but I am excited to build it out over time. Let me know if you have suggestions on how to improve the site, especially if you have any experience with webdev or graphic design!
