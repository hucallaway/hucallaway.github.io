Defend Planetary Science

NASA’s budget will be cut nearly in half, and we will destroy some of our most successful science probes right as they enter the best part of their cost curve. The tens of millions taken to design, manufacture, and launch a few thousand kilos of sensitive electronics out of Earth’s gravity well dwarfs the cost of reading the information these probes send back to us.

To show the universal support for NASA and its mission of scientific discovery, The Planetary Society is leading a global petition to Congress. Anyone can add their name.
