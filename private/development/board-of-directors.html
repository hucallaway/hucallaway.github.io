Why a Personal Board of Directors?

Every successful organization has a board of directors to provide guidance, oversight, and strategic direction. I've assembled my own personal board of directors - seven individuals whose wisdom, experience, and perspectives I value deeply. These mentors, friends, and thought leaders serve as my sounding board when I'm planning new projects, making important decisions, or seeking clarity on complex challenges. Each brings unique expertise and insights that help me think more strategically and avoid blind spots in my planning process.
